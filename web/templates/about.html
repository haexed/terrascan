Keep watch on our planet. Every day.

Terrascan makes complex environmental data accessible to everyone. We believe that understanding our planet's health should be as easy as checking the weather.

By combining real-time data from NASA, NOAA, and OpenAQ, we provide a unified view of global environmental conditions - from active fires and air quality to ocean temperatures and climate indicators.

‚ö° Technology

Terrascan is built with simplicity and reliability in mind:

  • Python + Flask - Lightweight web framework
  • PostgreSQL - Production database
  • Railway - Deployment platform
  • Leaflet.js - Interactive mapping
  • Bootstrap - Responsive design
  • Free APIs - No hosting costs for data, may change

The entire platform can run on a single server and requires no complex setup or cloud services.
